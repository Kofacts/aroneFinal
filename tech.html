Arones are designed to be fully autonomous, with artificial intelligence for safe navigation from take off, to delivery and landing. Arones use advanced deep learning algorithms to detect, recognize and avoid collision with other objects during flight.

Arone’s situation awareness allows them to predict attacks in other to navigate smartly during delivery.

Multiple safety measures have been implemented to ensure a safe Arone delivery. In the unlikely event where all of Arone’s rotors fail a parachute is deployed to allow for a gentle landing to ensure the safety of life and assets on the ground. Payloads are kept protected against rain fall and other hash weather conditions in an enclosed payload compartment and tethered down at the delivery location


Avports are a network of ground based battery swap stations, they are the equivalent of Airports for Arone delivery. They extend the distance Arones can travel and allow inter-state and inter-continental Arone deliveries.

Avports are 500 kilometers apart and besides being predominantly ground based can be built on Oceans such as the Atlantic Ocean to allow oversea and intercontinental Arone delivery.

Avports are powered solely using solar energy. Africa has an abundant supply of solar energy, this provides an eco friendly and low cost means to generate electrical energy required to power Arones. This also allows us to offer more affordable and competitive prices for personalized delivery.

Energy generated from Solar Panels at Avports is stored in arrays of batteries to be swapped into Arones when they land for recharge. Avports are the revolution to drone delivery.
